REHABILITATION PROGRAM

The Evoluciona IPS rehabilitation center in Bogotá offers a rehabilitation program for the consumption of psychoactive substances and negative behaviors based on humane and ethical principles. We have an interdisciplinary team of professionals specialized in the area of Psychology, Psychiatry, Occupational Therapy, Deportology, among others, who contribute to addressing the various difficulties of the person at the level of their emotional, behavioral, and cognitive spheres due to harmful use. of the consumption of psychoactives.



Our institutional objective at the level of rehabilitation for psychoactive substance use and behavior is to provide comprehensive support that allows the person to acquire functional life skills, enhance protective factors, acquire coping strategies in risk situations, identify and modify negative behaviors likewise provide intervention and accompaniment to the family nucleus.
