INTRAMURAL MODALITY

Our intramural modality is an individual process of change in addictive behavior. Under our accompaniment, the person works to identify the factors that maintain their difficulties, enhances awareness of the addiction disease and its associated behaviors, acquires, reinforces, and modifies behavior patterns, understands, and applies positive tools that allow them to face situations. complex in their daily lives, solve conflicts in their different areas, as well as maintain these changes once they graduate from the program.

Evoluciona IPS, has a management plan based on clinical interventions with scientific evidence at the individual, family and group level according to the structuring of a treatment according to the needs of the patient. It is constituted in therapeutic phases with established clinical objectives that begin with the adaptation to the process and acceptance of the user's problem, until its consolidation, detachment and adaptation to the family, social and educational environment.

The progress of each Stage is subject to the achievement of individual goals and achievements by the user according to the nature of the symptoms related to their consumption history and other associated pathologies.

INTRAMURAL SERVICES

Intramural program that includes:
1. Accommodation Service
2. Food Service
3. Patient Transport Service
4. Supply of medicines.
5. Continuous attention 24/7.
6. Specialized psychological intervention at the individual and family level.
7. Group therapeutic intervention in therapeutic community modality.
8. Accompaniment in reintegration therapy or adaptation to the environment (clinical modality day)
9. Psychiatry.
10. Neuropsychology (Remission determined by clinical criteria. Optional).
11. Occupational therapy.
12. Spiritual guidance.
13. Validation of Baccalaureate
14. Parent workshops.
15. Family groups.
16. Application of standardized tests for the detection of risk for drug use:

INTRAMURAL MODALITY CRITERIA

1. Patients aged between 13 to 25 years of the male gender.
2. Recognition of signs and symptoms of severe dependence on the substance, without self-control, unable to achieve abstinence on their own.
3. Denial of their problematic substance use condition, evidencing altered states, repeated non-compliance with rules in different contexts.
4. Significant deterioration and associated risk to the physical or mental health of oneself or third parties.
5. Profile that do not respond to outpatient treatment.
6. With mood disorders or other associated or generated from their addictive condition.
7. History of carrying out processes and treatments in an inpatient, outpatient, or day clinic modality that have been unsuccessful.
8. Psychopathological problems derived from addiction (Dual Pathology)

OUTPATIENT MODALITY

Modality made up of an interdisciplinary team of professionals in the Mental Health area, trained in addressing problems present in children, adolescents, adults, couples and families. At the level of outpatient or Mental Health modality, it includes various services, interventions with a clinical-care approach based on scientific evidence.

Our objective is based on the emotional and behavioral regulation of the person, their family environment and their vital areas in order to provide functional coping alternatives, self-control tools and promotion of the emotional health of our clients. 

OUTPATIENT MODALITY SERVICES

1. Individual Psychological Intervention
2. Family Psychological Intervention
3. Therapeutic Intervention.
4. Psychiatry
5. Neuropsychology (Remission determined by clinical criteria. Optional).
6. Standardized tests for the detection of psychoactive substances

OUTPATIENT MODALITY CRITERIA

1. Boys, girls, adolescents or adults with a high degree of risk for the start of consumption of psychoactive substances, or in an experimental phase.
2. Patients who, due to their addictive characteristics in frequency, intensity, magnitude, and duration, do not meet the criteria for the residential rehabilitation program.
3. They show behavioral difficulties in the family nucleus, repeated breach of rules at home, rebellion, defiant opposition, mythomania, aggressiveness, etc.
4. Partial recognition of their problems and dependence on the consumption of psychoactive substances and present a risk associated with their physical or mental health.
6. Commitment to abstain from consumption and consumption contexts, as well as intention to change.
7. Assistance to psychological therapies and compliance with established parameters.

MENTAL HEALTH MODALITY

Modality made up of an interdisciplinary team of professionals in the Mental Health area, trained in addressing problems present in children, adolescents, adults, couples and families. At the level of outpatient or Mental Health modality, it includes various services, interventions with a clinical-care approach based on scientific evidence.

Our objective is based on the emotional and behavioral regulation of the person, their family environment and in their vital areas in order to provide functional coping alternatives, self-control tools and promotion of the emotional health of our clients.

EXTERNAL CONSULTATION SERVICES

1. Individual Psychology
2. Family Psychology
3. Therapeutic Intervention.
4. Psychiatry
5. Neuropsychology (Optional)
6. ASSIST AND AUDIT detection tests for psychoactive substances

INTRAMURAL MODALITY CRITERIA

The program does not cover patients:
1. Severe mental disorder that prevents the personal autonomy of the patient, such as orientation in space and time.
2. Serious physical deterioration that prevents its treatment.
3. Catastrophic or terminal illness.
4. Irreversible cognitive limitations or mental retardation that make recovery difficult.
