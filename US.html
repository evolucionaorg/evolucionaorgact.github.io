WHO ARE WE?

We are a private entity, legally constituted and provider of Health Services - IPS, which works for the prevention and recovery of men and women, young people and adults with addiction and behavior problems, offering them a professional service, with defined intervention parameters. and high quality standards, where your values prevail, those of your family and above all the guarantee of receiving humane treatment.



EVOLUCIONA operates as IPS, level I of medium complexity, has legal status recognized by the District Health Department and its services duly authorized and certified by the District Health Department. EVOLUCIONA IPS is monitored by the Superintendency of Health in relation to the provision of the service.

Mission


Reconstruct the human fabric and social inclusion through highly updated models certified by global institutions where recovery programs, comprehensive prevention, education and research on psychoactive substances and associated problems are developed, using the right human resources in the different professional areas, to achieve efficiency and effectiveness in service provision.

Vision

Position Fundación Evoluciona as a leading foundation that, due to its intervention models, its quality of service and project facilitation, can achieve a suitable life model for our users, thus obtaining high recognition both nationally and internationally.
